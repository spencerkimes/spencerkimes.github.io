Itinerary

For our trip we decided to do a modified version of the short circuit pictured below. Unfortunately we were unable to make it to the northern half of the South Island and simply drove from Christchurch to Greymouth cutting across the island via Arthur's Pass. Luckily Arthur's Pass is incredibly beautiful. The stops were as follows:

  1. Christchurch - 1 night
  2. Fox Glacier - 2 nights
  3. Wanaka - 3 nights
  4. Te Anau - 4 nights
  5. Queenstown - 4 nights
  6. Tekapo - 3 nights
  7. Christchurch - 1 night
